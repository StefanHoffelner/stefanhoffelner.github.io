venue: 'Journal of Symbolic Logic 84(4), 1466--1483'

abstract: >-
  We show that, assuming the existence of the canonical inner model with one Woodin cardinal, there is a model of ZFC in which the nonstationary ideal on $\omega_1$ is saturated and the reals admit a projective well-order.
